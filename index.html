Intro

Once upon a time, there was a girl named Tracy, who was born in a small coastal city. Though the city is a peaceful, poetic and worth-living place, she always harbors a dream that one day she could set foot in the Digital Marketing jungle. Without hesitation, at the age of 20, she said goodbye to her family and began her journey to conquer the forest. Now, let's check out her journey right here.

The Journey

First, she made the decision to get through the Content Marketing waterfall to understand the bigger picture. While she was here, she had the best moments with three different communities including SEODO Agency, EcomMonster, and VnResource. Let's follow her progress through SEO-standard blog and social media posts below:

At SEODO Agency, she's well-trained to optimize the content for search engines and was assigned to optimize blog content of 5 projects about housewares, blockchain & crypto, fengshui, etc. If you're interested, spend a few minutes to have a look at some typical blogs that she wrote.

  • View Project

At EcomMonster, she was assigned to manage the blog content for Famiprints - a company's brand selling personalized products and gifts for anniversaries. She also took responsibility for designing product banners as well as images.

  • View Project

VnResource gave her a chance to work in a B2B company providing solutions for Human Resources and Education Business Management. Different from 2 positions above, the internship program allows her to take on the role of managing social contents and organizing internal events.

A year of experience in these villages brought out the best in her and helped her gain significant achievements.
